How to Use

Adding a Session

  1. Tap the + button in the Sessions tab
  2. Choose between Cash Game or Tournament
  3. Enter session details (stakes, buy-in, cash-out, location, etc.)
  4. Optionally track duration for hourly rate calculations
  5. Tap Save

Starting a Live Session

  1. Tap the + button and select Start Live Session
  2. Enter initial session details
  3. Tap Start Session to begin tracking
  4. Add additional buy-ins during play as needed
  5. When finished, tap End Session and enter your cash-out amount

Viewing Statistics

  • Go to the Statistics tab
  • Filter by All Games, Cash Games, or Tournaments
  • View overall performance, time-based results, and financial summaries
  • Analyze profit trends with interactive charts

Exporting Data

  1. Go to the Statistics tab
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear)
  3. Select Export Sessions as CSV
  4. Share the CSV files via email or save to files

Changing Language

  1. Go to the Statistics tab
  2. Tap the Settings icon
  3. Select your preferred language
  4. Tap Done to apply

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data safe?

Yes! All your data is stored locally on your device and automatically synced to your private iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

Can I use this app on multiple devices?

Yes! With iCloud sync enabled, your data automatically syncs across all your iOS devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

What happens if I delete the app?

If iCloud sync is enabled, your data is backed up in iCloud. When you reinstall the app, your data will be restored automatically.

Can I track multiple currencies?

Yes! Each session can be tracked in different currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, etc.).

How do I delete a session?

In the Sessions tab, swipe left on any session and tap Delete.

Why isn't my hourly rate showing?

Hourly rate is only calculated for sessions where you've tracked the duration. Make sure to enable Track Duration when adding a session.
